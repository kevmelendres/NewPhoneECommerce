FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Is the website updated?
Yes updated pricing and availability. Orders are subject for confirmation or clearance after checkout if in case there is a website availability bug.
What are Overseas Stocks?
Stocks from abroad. Local brand warranty thru our fulfillment may still apply. If local stocks arrived earlier than order from abroad, we switch the availability status to domestic and ship the order from domestic stocks.
What are Domestic Stocks?
Stocks currently available within local showroom, warehouse and distributors. If sold out local, we switch availability to overseas or out of stock.
What are Critical Stocks?
Stocks sold out or below 1-2 items available. We allocate this for buffer stocks [we use this stocks for warranty replacement] or the stock is not yet available for shipping due to courier availability ie Tubes beyond 1 meter is not available to ship via courier available and Furnitures can be shipped via trucking only.
How is Cash on Delivery?
Our cash on delivery is nationwide for up to Php 30,000.00 amount except large packaging items like monitor, chassis, laptops and furnitures. If not available you may consider our partial payment and full payment via cash on delivery. Thank you
How long is the delivery timeframe?
Delivery timeframe may vary on stock status. You may check the estimated delivery timeframe before add to cart button.
  • Multiple item orders for PC Build may take longer than usual or 1-3 business days longer than estimated delivery timeframe. This is due to assembly, testing and more complex packaging process.
  • Laptop units with requested applications may take longer than usual by 1-2 business days longer than estimated timeframe.
  • Orders in some parts of Visayas and Mindanao may take longer than usual or around 1-3 business days longer than estimated delivery timeframe.
